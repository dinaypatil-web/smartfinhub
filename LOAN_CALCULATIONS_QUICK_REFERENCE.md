### EMI Formula
```
Monthly Payment = [Principal Ã— Rate Ã— (1+Rate)^Months] / [(1+Rate)^Months - 1]
```

### Example
- **Loan**: $300,000
- **Rate**: 6.5% per year
- **Term**: 30 years (360 months)
- **EMI**: $1,896.20/month

### Interest Calculation
```
Daily Interest = (Balance Ã— Annual Rate) / 365
Accrued Interest = Sum of daily interest from start to today
```

## ğŸ” Understanding Your Numbers

### EMI Components
- **Principal**: Reduces loan balance
- **Interest**: Cost of borrowing

### Early Payments
- More interest, less principal
- Example: $1,896 = $1,625 interest + $271 principal

### Later Payments
- Less interest, more principal
- Example: $1,896 = $271 interest + $1,625 principal
