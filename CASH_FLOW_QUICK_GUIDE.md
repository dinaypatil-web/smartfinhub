## ğŸ“ Common Questions

**Q: Why is my closing balance different from my account balance?**
A: The opening balance is calculated from account balances at report generation time, not at the start of the month. For accurate month-end reconciliation, compare the closing balance with your actual account balance on the last day of the month.

**Q: Are loan accounts included in the opening balance?**
A: No, loan accounts are liabilities (money you owe), not assets. They're excluded from opening/closing balance calculations but loan payments are tracked in Financing Activities.

**Q: What if a month has no transactions?**
A: The cash flow statement will still show, with all activity categories at zero, and closing balance equal to opening balance.

**Q: Can I compare multiple months?**
A: Currently you can view one month at a time, but you can switch between months to compare. Future versions may include multi-month comparisons.
